ORDER

Without law, you are nothing more than the animals. Slave to Disorder. Law
and Order are the only thing that raises us above them, good and evil does
not matter; all are the same before it. Ruling with an iron fist or
creating a civilization that favors neither good nor evil, the law is a tool
to control, to protect, and to gain. Use it at your whim, but do not falter.

The world is ripe with disorder. Order is the shield that protects the
world's population from destruction. Before it's blind eye, all are equal.
The immoral murderer and the pious preacher; all will benefit, and heed,
it's guidelines. It favors nothing, but elevates itself above all. It
looks down on none, but is superior to everything else. It is the only
thing that matters. But, just as laws are tools, so shall people use them
to their advantage. Many who live the Way of Order know what they may or
may not be able to get away with, and often corrupt the law to further their
own goals, be it for good or evil.

[ LAW ] - The Pillar of Law

The cabal of Law is perhaps the most visible, revered, and hated
cabal in Mirlan. They are the servants of the society, the judges,
jury, and executioners of those who would walk a path that would be
contrary to public good. They are devoted and stalwart servants,
and have a keen and sharp understanding of the laws of the nations,
as well as a thick skin to battle off naysayers.

The Pillar of Law is comprised of two separate clans:

The Enforcers - These lawmen chase fugitives, and sentence
The Magistrates - These lawmen are protectors of the homeland

The Pillar of Law is a highly sought after cabal, one that requires
cunning, speed, diligence, and intellect. As such, only those who
are law abiding citizens, and not natively forest denizens may
apply to the Law cabal.

If you are interested in applying to the Pillar of Law, submit
your application to LAW.
