BARD

Bards are the minstrels, poets, tarot readers and storytellers of the lands.
With their instruments and songs, they are able to weave wondrous workings
upon the spirit and their surroundings. A bard is one who seeks the company
of others for mirth and merry. Their melodies can quicken one's feet for
battle, provide nourishment and drink for entire gatherings, or be a lullaby
to soft as to put anyone to sleep. While not as proficient in the arts of
war as warriors, there are known to be accomplished barroom brawlers, able
break a man's jaw with a single blow or use their playing cards as weapons.

See also: HELP REHEARSE, REFRAIN, SONG
