FERAL

Through the taint of magic from a long since forgotten Arch-Mage a tribe of
of people who lived not too far from the mages strong hold were used in
experiments of this evil lord.

After hundreds of years of genetic manipulation, torture and mental assault
on the minds of these people their bodies and minds began to evolve into a
more animalistic form. With the physical change came mental changes as well.

These people once known as the Ferishalli became known simply as "The Feral."

A strong leader came to sit on the Feral's council and convinced the Feral to
rise up against this dark arch mage and free themselves from his slavery and
torture. The struggle was monumental. Lasting but a few short hours the
Feral eventually overcame the arch mage at a high price. Nearly all had been
destroyed and nearly wiped out completely. The battle took men, women and
children alike. Leaving but a few of the younger of the tribe behind.

Soon after the battle, the tribe disbanded. Heated arguments over how best
to run the tribe and who would be the new leaders broke this once strong and
ancient people up. Now, over the hundreds of years of freedom, Feral have
become very bent on keeping their individual freedoms. Tribe mentality all
but washed from their way of thinking.

Feral can be the following classes:

Warrior, Ranger, Thief, Cleric, and Shaman
