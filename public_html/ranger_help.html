RANGER RANGERS

Rangers are the men and women who dedicated their life to study of the land
outside the cities. They are often trackers and hunters who offer their skills
to those who are less skilled in outdoor environments leading exploring parties
deep into wilds of the land. They are very adequate fighters with skills to
use the outdoor terrain to their advantage as well as some ability to dabble in
magic. Rangers are able to blend in with his surroundings as well as move
quietly in the forests. They also have been known to beckon the help the
creatures in the forest, seeking them to come to his aid or sending swarms of
insects to plague their enemies.

They are dedicated to art of fired weapons such as bows as no other profession
is, and late in their career are able to specialize by selecting an area of
interest from Ranger Lore. Much like warriors they are also capable of
excelling in various weapon types, thus being able to select two weapon types
during their career with which to be come experts.

HUMAN and LYCANTHROPE rangers of exceptional dedication to the cause of nature
may choose to joins the ranks of the Druids. Those who are interested must be
of the 30th rank, and upon reaching it, must inquire at the Druid's Guild.
